Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses work best?

Any establishment that values creating memorable customer experiences! We can create custom shows for restaurants, bars, cafes, retail stores, event venues, and more. If you have foot traffic and want to stand out, we can design something perfect for your space.

Can I customize the show concept?

Absolutely! Every show is custom-designed around your business, brand, and goals. We work closely with you to ensure the performance aligns perfectly with your vision and enhances your establishment's unique character.

Why should I trust a new service?

Fair question! Plot Swap combines years of game design experience with theatrical performance. As a game designer, I know how to create experiences that engage people and keep them talking. Now I'm applying those same principles to help businesses create unforgettable moments. Plus, we offer a free consultation where we'll design your show concept before you commit to anything.

How far in advance should I book?

For One and Done shows, we recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance. For Full Productions, we suggest starting the conversation 6-9 months before your desired launch date to allow for the complete development process.

Do you disrupt normal business operations?

For One and Done shows, performances happen during your regular hours and blend seamlessly with your normal operations. For Full Productions, shows run during closed hours, so there's zero impact on your day-to-day business.

What makes this different from traditional marketing?

Traditional marketing talks at people. We design experiences people want to talk about. Using game design principles (memorable moments, emotional engagement, shareable stories), we create marketing that doesn't feel like marketing. Your customers become your storytellers.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out the contact form below! We'll schedule a free 30-minute consultation to discuss your vision, answer any questions, and design a show concept. If you're not excited about what we create together, walk away. No hard feelings, no commitment.
