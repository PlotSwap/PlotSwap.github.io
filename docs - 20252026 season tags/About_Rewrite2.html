The Craft

Plot Swap is an exercise in experiential design. I research, write, and direct scripted plays that hide in the real world. It’s theater staged where life is already happening: an overly confident third-date proposal, a best man speech that turns into a confession, or a painting class with a teacher’s pet crush.

The craft is in the build:
Curiosity earns attention. Attention invites connection. Connection unlocks emotion. Emotion becomes memory.
"Real world entertainment deserves another chance."
RUSSELL VARNER // HOUSTON

Meet the Designer

I'm a Universal Designer. Most days, I solve complex design problems for video games and digital platforms. But I've always known that the most important interface is the one between two human beings.

My restlessness started as a kid with an overactive imagination that often got me into trouble. Today, I use that same energy to unlock the hidden potential in ordinary spaces. I don't have a giant production budget or a marketing team. It is just me, a few dedicated actors, and a belief that human connection is the ultimate design challenge.

If you've ever looked at a local business or a quiet city street and wondered, "What if this could be more?" – then you and I speak the same language.
