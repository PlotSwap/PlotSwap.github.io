Scripted stories hiding in public

[ Houston, TX ]

What We Do

We create immersive theater performances that hide in plain sight. Real actors. Real locations. Unsuspecting audiences who stumble into a story already in progress.

The goal? To make the world feel a little less predictable. To reward the curious. To prove that magic still exists. You just have to pay attention.

Meet Russell Varner

A Lifelong Storyteller

I'm a Universal Designer who sees potential where others see the ordinary. As a kid, my storytelling got me in trouble. As an adult, it became my superpower.

I can't help but reimagine everything. Turning a farmer's market stall into theater, a wedding into an experience, a simple party into a memory people talk about for years. Whether I'm designing countless video games, crafting immersive performances, or building social experiments that blur the line between art and life, I'm always asking the same question: What if this could be more?

Punch Up Real Life.

Why We Keep Secrets

The best experiences can't be explained. They have to be felt. So we don't do traditional marketing. No spoilers. No hype videos. No algorithm-optimized content.

You'll discover us the old-fashioned way: through a friend, a flyer, or sheer curiosity. Because analog entertainment deserves another chance.

Support the Mission
