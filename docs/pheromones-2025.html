THE DEBRIEF

What We Learned

The format is a tool. The feelings are the point.

What hit

The format landed. People liked making the calls, and the room energy changed the night in a way you can’t fake. The story held up across paths. The cast proved they could pivot on cue without losing truth.

What surprised us

The hardest choices weren’t “plot” choices. They were emotional ones. When the room controls how a character feels, the audience gets weirdly invested fast.

What we’re fixing next

Immersion wasn’t consistent. The distance between “watching” and “being inside it” still showed. That’s the gap we care about closing.

Challenge accepted.
